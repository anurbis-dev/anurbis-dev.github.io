Touch gestures

  • 1 finger — paint / pan with active tool
  • 1 finger, hold still — temporarily switches to eraser until release
  • 2 fingers on canvas — pinch to zoom, twist to rotate, drag to pan
  • 2 fingers on a panel — resize that panel
  • 3 fingers swipe — left: undo, right: redo
  • 4 fingers drag — shift active layer pixels (disable in Settings → gestures to restore tab swipe)
  • 5 fingers swipe — toggle fullscreen
Drag a panel near a screen edge to dock it. Hold a layer name to rename it. Middle-click drag (or LMB on empty panel space) scrolls panels. Hotkeys: Settings → Hotkeys.
